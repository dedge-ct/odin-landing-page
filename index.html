A Page about Golden Retrievers
Golden Retrievers are a medium-sized retrieving breed from Scotland known for their gentle nature and silky golden coat. They are also the best bois.
Why Golden Retrievers?
They're cute af.
They're relatively easy to train.
They are outgoing and like to party.
They get along with their hooman.
Once someone has had good fortune to share a true love affair with a Golden Retriever, one’s life and one’s outlook is never quite the same.
- Betty White
